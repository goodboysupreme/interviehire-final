# Product

## Register

product

## Users
HR managers, recruiters, and hiring teams who need to manage workflows, track analytics, and configure AI-driven hiring tools efficiently.

## Product Purpose
To provide a centralized, efficient, and visually refined portal for managing the entire hiring lifecycle, from job postings to AI-driven candidate evaluation (AI Swarm), team access, and usage analytics.

## Brand Personality
Sophisticated, precise, and fluid. The interface should feel like a high-end, modern tool—calm, focused, and effortlessly powerful. 

**Anti-references**: Cluttered, dated enterprise software (like legacy CRMs), overly playful/chaotic consumer apps, or generic "SaaS cream" templates.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration**: Every visual element must serve a functional purpose. Data and actions should be instantly scannable.
2. **Fluid efficiency**: Interactions should feel seamless and responsive, reducing friction in complex, multi-step workflows.
3. **Calm authority**: The interface should inspire confidence through clean data presentation, predictable behavior, and refined motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard usability best practices with a focus on clear visual hierarchy and readable contrast. Strict WCAG compliance is not a current blocker, but baseline readability and keyboard navigability should be maintained.
